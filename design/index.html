Design rationale

How I translated TailGrids onto QuikFill — and the rules that keep the two surfaces consistent.

Why this looks like TailGrids

TailGrids' signature is a confident indigo on calm neutrals, generous whitespace, soft multi-layer shadows and gently rounded cards. I kept the shadcn token names the repo already uses, but re-pointed their values:

  • Primary — #3056D3, used for actions, links, focus and active nav. Never as a giant background fill.
  • Neutrals — TailGrids' #637381 body on #DFE4EA strokes; #1C274C ink for headings.
  • Shadows — soft, low-opacity card shadows carry elevation instead of heavy borders.

Two surfaces, one kit

The extension and dashboard share the exact same tokens and component classes, so a button or badge looks identical in both.

  • Density: balanced — comfortable in the dashboard, compact enough for a 360px side panel.
  • Plus Jakarta Sans across both; JetBrains Mono only for field values, selectors and seeds.
  • Lucide icons, outline, 1.5px — matching the repo's shadcn-vue (new-york) setup.
  • Light + dark from one class on <html>; toggle it top-right.
